About Me

Adventurous Determined Analytical

Maya Renee is a 27 year old UX/UI designer based in Texas. Her journey began in October of 2022 after coming to realize she needed a change in her life. She always had a desire for tech,
but never crossed the threshold until now! After seeking a certificate in UX/UI Design, she now specializes in mobile app and website design.
Maya also spent time earning a Bachelor of Arts in Modern Language Studies with a Minors in East Asian Studies; Linguistics due to her love of languages and culture.
“More black women are needed in tech and as a result I am consistently doing my best to make that a reality.”
