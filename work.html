DuoQ Gaming App

"Don’t take the L!”
DuoQ is an interactive platonic matchmaking app that provides the user the ability to research and choose a gaming partner to be their DuoQ in any game of their choice. DuoQ allows the user to have an online gaming experience or seek out local players to create tabletop gaming sessions.
